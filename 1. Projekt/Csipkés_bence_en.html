I have a black-and-white cat, he's called Bolyhos.

Adopted cat, we found him on the street in the winter of 2020. We made him grow to us easily, we just needed to give him food. His last owner probably died and noone cared for him, because he's a very smart housecat.



Traits

His fur isn't completely uniform, as he doesn't always clean himself, because he's old and lazy.

He's a very gluttonous, a little eating machine. His face is always a mess after eating, which he happily uses to rub against people.

By now he became very accustomed, and he always waits by the door to greet us.


Feeding

Since he's quite gluttonous, he eats anything. Usually we give them the stuff below.


Outside food Outside treat
Felix Shah Sensitive
Gourmet Paste Coshida Pure Taste

Bathing

He's an outdoors cat, so we don't bathe him, he's a clean animal anyways.

However it's interesting, that he's very leasurely and old, so he sometimes leaves out cleaning himself.


Training

The cat is mostly an untrainable selfish animal. He only ever flatters people when he's hungry, but he can be cute and playful as well. He can be trained for some stuff if he has enough morivation ex: don't scratch us since my sister likes to play rough with him, or give handshakes when you put your hand out for him.


Conditions of keeping

In my oppinion a cat doesn't belong indoors, Because it makes fur go everywhere, claws up everything and makes his bussiness in the wrong place.

We put together a well insulated cathouse for him that he can crawl into whenever
